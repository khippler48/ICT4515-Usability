Farmington Hills Firefighters Association Local 2659

The Farmington Hills Fire Departments provides fire and emergency medical services (EMS) as well as fire prevention and safety education, and Fire Code enforcement. Services are provided by firefighters who operate out of five fire stations strategically located throughout the community. Stations #1, #2, #4, and #5, are staffed 24 hours a day, seven days a week with Station #3 being staffed 7:30 AM to Midnight seven days a week.
